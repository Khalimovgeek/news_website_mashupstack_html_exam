Mohanlal Confirmed in Prithviraj's "Khalifa" After Empuraan

Mohanlal has officially been confirmed as part of the cast in Khalifa, a film starring and directed by Prithviraj Sukumaran. This collaboration comes after their much-awaited project Empuraan, the sequel to the blockbuster Lucifer. Fans are excited to witness the powerful duo once again on screen.

Khalifa is expected to feature Mohanlal in a significant role, adding depth and star power to the project. Although detailed plot information is currently under wraps, industry insiders suggest that the film will explore intense themes and strong character arcs. The announcement has generated massive excitement among Malayalam cinema fans.
